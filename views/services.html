Services
BudgetLive will guide you in building your personalize budget. We
manage you budget on a time frame that you chose between two weeks
and one month. A total amount for the time frame will be divided into
personalized categories, where you can choose how to divide your
money. For each category you can easily log how much you spent.
You can view how much you spent in your overall budget and how much
you spent in each category. We allow you to link another person into
your budget. You can view how much spent as well what they spent specifically.
You can see all the numbers and graphs whenever you want. It's so easy
to manage, use, and become addicted to.
